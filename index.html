The Golden Gate Bridge is a suspension bridge across the place the where San Francisco Bay opens into the Pacific Ocean, so-called Golden Gate (hence the name). It connects San Francisco with Marin County and it is 2737 meters long and 227 meters high. Some 45 million of vehicles cross Golden Gate Bridge in a year. Golden Gate Bridge is one of the landmarks of San Francisco and, until 1964, it was a bridge with longest suspension bridge main span in the world.
