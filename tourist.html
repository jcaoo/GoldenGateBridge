Tourist Information

The Golden Gate Bridge is a tourist attraction. The bridge does not only connect San Francisco to California northern Counties but is one of the most visited tourist destinations for its serene beauty, architecture, and nearby fun-filled activities.

    You can also gaze at the Golden Gate Bridge from these locations below:
  • Fort Point National Historic Site
  • Baker Beach
  • Fort Baker
  • Crissy Field
  • Battery East Vista
  • Lands End
  • Golden Gate Overlook
